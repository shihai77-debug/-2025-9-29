# EP20 CUT-04 Reaction Video Lesson

Use this note when generating EP20 CUT-04 style reaction videos.

Source role:
- Use final_cut_source_index for duration, dialogue, BGM, SFX, continuity.
- Use storyboard board only for reaction order, blocking, framing, and spatial relation.
- Use character reference sheets for faces, clothing, body shape, and temperament.

CUT-04 production lesson:
- This CUT is useful as a quiet reaction video node.
- Keep it simple: no dialogue, no large movement, no new plot action.
- 0-3s: focus on Hei Qi, one small black fox, only slight ear movement.
- 3-8s: Tan Que glances from Hei Qi to Lu Chenzhou.
- Lu already has the documents and remains seated.
- Do not show document handoff in this CUT.
- Do not make Hei Qi a wolf, dog, human, or monster.
- Do not let Tan Que become Bai Qingtan or wear white clothes.
- Do not add Shen Bochuan, Han Zhixuan, extra people, subtitles, arrows, storyboard borders, or black-and-white storyboard style in the final video.
- Keep background quiet, low-saturation, and secondary to characters.

Recommended prompt strategy:
- Main visible subjects: Hei Qi and Tan Que.
- Lu Chenzhou may appear seated with documents as continuity, but he should not take over the shot.
- Use fixed camera, stable medium shot or medium-close shot.
- No dialogue. Use very low strings and wind only.
